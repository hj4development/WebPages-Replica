PPA.WORLD - FOOTBALL AGENT

Founded in 1978 by Mr. Paskal Papadopoulos, PPA.WORLD is the leading sport management company in its’ territories, representing international football players worldwide.

Headquartered in Dubai UAE the company also maintains European offices in Cyprus. Started as a football agency, PPA.WORLD has expanded its’ business activities in all kind of sports areas.
